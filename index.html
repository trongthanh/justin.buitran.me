hello

I‘m Justin Bui Tran, an experienced art director and senior web designer. Digital media is my passion, and I always try to focus my abilities on meeting the clients’ needs, the direction their companies are heading and the successes they can achieve.
I believe that ideas and concepts can come from everywhere and anywhere – that creativity naturally comes from an open mind. Maybe that’s why I love to explore different corners and angles of view in life, and see where they lead.
In addition to art and design, travelling, nature and sport are all very important to me. When I was a child, I always dreamed about a place where we could live, walk and play among the clouds. In my dreams it was always a bright and colourful world, close to nature and fresh air. Always a refreshing escape from the ordinary. Welcome to my world…

intel

From 2011 to the present much of my work is for Intel, one of the most successful technology companies in the world, for numerous campaigns spread across Southeast Asia. Intel is always challenging, always interesting. The target audience is often very broad, and not restricted to any particular age or level of technological knowledge. My work needs to cover every kind of audience and user experience. And of course, when delivering ideas to the client, everything needs to follow very strict guidelines.

zorok

Zorok Mini Champion Cup, a campaign for Zorok (Vietnam) beer, was developed to support and encourage participation in Vietnam’s growing football clubs by finding young talent. (As well as raise awareness of Zorok beer and have it associated with football.) I was responsible for the digital campaign, which mainly focussed on driving the campaign traffic to Zorok’s microsite and social media networks. check it out ->

mon wine

To build the brand for the Mon Win wine shop, the idea was to reinforce the way wine lets you express your individual tastes, and is best enjoyed with great friends and good food. The emphasis was on creating an impression of natural enjoyment, a soft and calm feeling, while increasing the desire to be with your friends and chill out with a bottle from Mon Wine.

vncreative group

Creatives love to share their work. To see who dares to be different, who pushes the limits of what is possible to create, who proudly calls what work their own something they are proud to call their own. And of course, creatives love to see how they stack up against their peers. Vn Creative was designed to connect people living and working in the creative field. Although they’re a group with an eye on Vietnam, VN Creative has no geographical boundaries.

check it out ->

vinamit

This website design was to launch the drink Tomeli, a new brand by Vinamit featuring various delicious flavours. The idea was to try and give people the feeling they were out in the countryside and on the dairy farm, since natural and farm-fresh were key USPs to engage people increasingly looking for healthy food options due to their less-than-healthy urban lifestyles. check it out ->

vespa

To build Vespa’s brand perception as the only stylish (and most reliable) option in Vietnam, a country where the streets are literally clogged with small-bore motorcycles, a most stylish route was chosen. Fashion. Glamour. Icon. Your partner on the road, wherever that road may take you. check it out ->

miller beer

Street food and beer is an integral part of Vietnamese culture. Get together after work, chill out, share funny stories, catch up on current gossip and lament the latest sport news. The idea was to create a Miller-Zorok Football community where people could connect, share fun stories and exchange knowledge about football with each other... and the rest of the world.
